Before the first summit

Four finance ministers first tested the smaller room.

Shultz’s memoir traces a crucial precursor to a private March 1973 meeting in the White House Library. Japan joined that finance-minister circle later in 1973; a separate leaders’ track began with six countries in 1975 and became seven in 1976.

Scope that can be trusted

Every annual leaders-level record, with the institutional changes left visible.

This collection follows one continuous series: the G6 in 1975, the G7 from 1976, the G8 years, the suspended 2014 Sochi meeting and replacement Brussels G7, and the unusual 2020 U.S. virtual presidency. It does not silently treat ministerial meetings as leaders’ summits.
